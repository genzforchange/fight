ICE OUT OF LA

To carry out their illegal, inhumane attack on Los Angeles, ICE is spending millions of your taxpayer dollars putting their agents up in hotels across the city. They’re not welcome here.

That’s why we made an easy-to-use tool to demand that hotels refuse to house ICE agents. It’s never been easier to fight back.

How It Works

Set Up (iOS)

  1. Before You Begin:

    Open this page in an external browser like Safari or Chrome. The Shortcut will not work correctly in in-app browsers like TikTok. Also, make sure you’re using an Apple device with the Mail app configured.

  2. Download the Shortcut:

    Click the Apple Shortcut and press “Set Up Shortcut”.

  3. Follow setup prompts and allow permissions:

    Follow the prompts in the Shortcuts app. You may be asked to add the shortcut to your library and confirm when prompted.

    The Shortcut will also request permission to send emails and show notifications. Tap “Allow” or “Always Allow” to enable functionality.

Create an App Automation

  1. Create a new automation:

    Tap the Automation icon at the bottom of your screen, then hit the to start a new one.

  2. Search and Select “App” trigger:

    Choose an App you use daily (ie TikTok, Instagram) and hit "Done"

  3. Set Up the Trigger:

    Ensure that App "is Opened" is selected
    Optionally enable “Run Immediately” so you don’t have to confirm each day.

  4. Select the ICE OUT OF LA Shortcut:

    Once you do this, it should auto-save and take you back to the Automation menu. You're good to go!

Set Up (Android)

  1. Before You Begin:

    You’ll need a Google Account and the Gmail app installed and signed in on your device.

  2. Download Automate:

    Grab the Automate app by LlamaLab from the Play Store: Install Automate.

  3. Import the Flow:

    Click here to download our custom flow within Automate.

    Select the GZ4C-FIGHT Flow on the menu and continue to follow the prompts.

  4. Select and Review Privileges:

    Check the following boxes:

    • appear on top of other apps or parts of the screen
    • access to manage all files
    • ignore app hibernation
    • ignore battery optimizations

Create a Daily Trigger

  1. Start Flow:

    2. After granting permissions, select "Start". Follow the prompts and select a Google account.

  2. Select a time:

    3. Set your preferred send time (e.g. 9:00 AM daily) for the emails to send. You're all set!
